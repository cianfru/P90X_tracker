Standard chin-ups (dots = per session, solid line = yearly median) sit flat at ~20 for seven years. The dashed line is the harder L-sit / X (wide-leg) variant, steady at ~15. The early-2019 travel sessions on elastic bands are excluded so the baseline is true bar work.
